name: discovery interview
description: Interview script design using Mom Test methodology
---

Run the Interview exercise from `~/.claude/skills/discovery/SKILL.md` (Stage 1: User Interviews).

1. Ask what product/service the user is building and who their target users are
2. Generate a custom Mom Test interview script with:
   - Warm-up questions tailored to their ICP
   - Problem exploration questions specific to their domain
   - Solution validation section (if they have a prototype)
   - Commitment extraction questions
3. Include a tailored question bank drawn from the SKILL.md templates
4. Flag anti-patterns specific to their situation
5. Recommend sample size based on their research goal (problem vs solution vs pricing validation)
6. Provide a post-interview synthesis template they can copy

Output format: Complete interview script ready to print and use in the next conversation.
