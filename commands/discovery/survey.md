description: Survey design with bias prevention and sample size guidance
---

Run the Survey Design exercise from `~/.claude/skills/discovery/SKILL.md` (Stage 2: Survey Design).

1. Ask what the user wants to learn and who the target respondents are
2. Design a complete survey following the SKILL.md template:
   - Screening questions to filter for ICP
   - Behavior questions (what they actually do)
   - Pain point questions (open text + ranking)
   - Willingness questions (Sean Ellis PMF question included by default)
   - Demographics (only if needed for segmentation)
3. Run the bias prevention checklist on every question
4. Calculate the sample size target based on their population
5. Suggest distribution channels matched to their audience
6. Keep the survey under 5 minutes / 15 questions max

Output format: Copy-pastable survey with all questions, answer options, and a distribution plan.
